About Simmer

Simmer is an open-source recipe manager built to help you organize, discover, and share your favorite dishes. Whether you're cataloging family heirlooms or experimenting with new cuisines, it keeps everything in one place.

No ads. No 47-paragraph life stories before the recipe. No cookie banners begging for your data. Just cooking.
